First Jobzz




First Jobzz provides a sure way to success in an intensely competitive professional world through a well researched and path breaking career grooming package. First Jobzz enables students to understand their inner strengths and abilities and trains them to use these to their advantage. The adoption of a clear, precise and an achievable Career Goal in line with their inherent skills and aptitude, is thus enabled.

First Jobzz offers the complete talent lifecycle ecosystem of assessment, skill enhancement and industry partnership for talent absorption. With top professionals as mentors and customized career programs, the students become workplace ready and meet the emerging industry challenges with ease. In addition, confidence, Self-belief and an awareness of the professional world are inculcated to success in their chosen careers.
